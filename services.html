What’s included

01

Strategy

We start with the why and the who.

Discovery calls, audience and goal clarity, content structure, and information architecture. You get a clear brief and sitemap so design and development have a solid foundation. No building in the dark.

  • Discovery & goals
  • Content strategy
  • Information architecture
  • Brief & sitemap
02

Design

Visual identity and interfaces that convert.

High-fidelity UI in Figma: typography, color, spacing, and components. Motion and interaction are part of the design, not an afterthought. You get files that are development-ready and a design system that scales.

  • UI / UX design
  • Visual identity
  • Motion & interaction
  • Design system

Development

Built for speed, accessibility, and the long term.

Semantic HTML, maintainable CSS, and JavaScript or React where it adds value. We focus on Lighthouse scores, accessibility (WCAG), and clean code. Static sites, CMS-backed, or custom apps—all deployed and ready to hand off or maintain.

Our process

From first call to launch. Basic: ~1 week; Pro: ~2 weeks; fully custom we scope together.

  1. 1

    Discovery

    Kickoff call, goals, audience, and content. We align on scope and timeline. You get a written brief.

    1–2 days
  2. 2

    Strategy & structure

    Sitemap, content outline, and key pages. We lock the foundation before design starts.

    3–5 days
  3. 3

    Design

    Visual design in Figma. We present, you review. Up to 2 revision rounds included (Pro).

    1–2 weeks
  4. 4

    Build

    Development with regular check-ins. You see the site in progress and can test early.

    1–2 weeks
  5. 5

    Launch

    Final QA, performance pass, and go-live. We hand off assets and docs. Optional support retainer.

    1–2 days
