What’s included

Strategy

  • Information architecture
  • Brief & sitemap

Design

High-fidelity UI in Figma: typography, color, spacing, and components. Motion and interaction are part of the design, not an afterthought. You get files that are development-ready and a design system that scales.

Our process

From first call to launch. Typically 4–6 weeks for a Pro project; Starter and Full vary by scope.

  1. 1

    Discovery

    Kickoff call, goals, audience, and content. We align on scope and timeline. You get a written brief.

    1–2 days
  2. 2

    Strategy & structure

    Sitemap, content outline, and key pages. We lock the foundation before design starts.

    3–5 days
  3. 3

    Design

    Visual design in Figma. We present, you review. Up to 2 revision rounds included (Pro).

    1–2 weeks
  4. 4

    Build

    Development with regular check-ins. You see the site in progress and can test early.

    1–2 weeks
  5. 5

    Launch

    Final QA, performance pass, and go-live. We hand off assets and docs. Optional support retainer.

    1–2 days
